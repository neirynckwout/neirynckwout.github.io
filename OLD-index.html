dino

Dino is condensed, bold, geometric. Inspired by road- signature fonts like DIN and interstate. But tries to be more human-like with a double story 'a' and double story 'g'. Dino also has some funny icons.
Check dino here!

thank you for cycling

The big amount of car traffic is one part of the current mobility problems in Brussels. This car-traffic has a lot of negative effects on local health, the environment and the usage of the limited space in the city. We would be much better off, if we change from car traffic to bike-traffic.

wakey wakey

Wakey wakey is a concept app for a personalisable alarm clock. As a user you choose what you want to hear before you’re being awaked, when you have to wake up, and when you really got out of bed.

book series

A series of books with folk tales from all around the world. The concept of the series is that every book has the same looking cover, size and typography, but his own color in the inside and on the backcover. The books seem very serene with some playfull accents.
